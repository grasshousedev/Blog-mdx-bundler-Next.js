---
title: Setup JSX support in emmet
category: Snippets
description: Add JSX support to emmet for auto complete
published: true
---

You might need to add this additional step to your VS-Code settings to support JSX in VS-Code, even though Emmet is natively supported out of the box

```json
{
    "emmet.includeLanguages": {
    "javascript": "javascriptreact"
  },
}
```
